Unit Converter guidance

Perform fast conversions across engineering units with Pint-backed accuracy, interval support, and expression evaluation.

Using the workspace

  1. Pick a unit family (length, pressure, thermal conductivity, etc.). Only compatible units are listed.
  2. Enter a numeric value; decimal and scientific notation are both accepted. Interval mode keeps temperature offsets intact.
  3. Optional: define significant figures, decimal precision, or switch to scientific/engineering notation.
  4. Submit the form to see the converted value plus the base SI quantity used for verification.

Expression evaluator

The expression panel accepts compound calculations such as 5 kJ/mol to eV or 980 cm^3 * 7 g/cm^3. If the expression omits a to <unit> clause, supply the target unit separately. Formatting controls mirror the main converter.

Reference & accuracy

  • Unit definitions are sourced from pint==0.23 with additional engineering aliases defined in plugins/unit_converter/core/registry.py.
  • Temperature interval conversions leverage Pint's delta units (e.g., delta_degC) to avoid offset drift.
  • Electron volt ↔ molar energy conversions use Avogadro's constant with double precision and round-half-up formatting.
